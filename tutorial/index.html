How to Buy $FLXCAT

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to buy $FLXCAT:

  1. Download and install Phantom or Solflare wallet
  2. Go to Pump.fun
  3. Click on "Connect to a wallet" and select Phantom/Solflare
  4. Enter the amount of $FLXCAT you want to buy and click on "Place Trade"
  5. Wait for the transaction to be confirmed and check your wallet application for your $FLXCAT tokens
